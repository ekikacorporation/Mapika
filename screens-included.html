Screens and Layout

The "Mapika" Electric Vehicle (EV) Location Finder Mobile App UI Kit is meticulously designed to offer a seamless and visually appealing experience on mobile devices. With the layout tailored to the iPhone 13 Mini's screen dimensions (375 x 812 pixels), this UI Kit ensures compatibility and optimal performance on a variety of mobile screens. Here, we delve deeper into the design choices, fonts, images, and icons used to craft this remarkable resource.

Layout Designed for iPhone 13 Mini: The choice of the iPhone 13 Mini's layout as the foundation for the "Mapika" UI Kit is strategic and forward-thinking. This layout caters to the smallest smartphone screen size in recent trends, which ensures that the UI Kit can be easily adapted to fit various mobile screens. By starting with a compact layout, designers and developers have the flexibility to scale and customize the app for larger devices while maintaining a consistent design language.

Screen Size and Scroll Behavior: The screen size of 375 x 812 pixels offers a compact canvas for designing the app's interface. However, it's important to note that in some screens, the height is intentionally extended to showcase scrolling behavior and elements that become visible after scrolling. This design approach not only optimizes the use of screen real estate but also enhances the user experience by revealing additional content when needed.

Fonts: Typography plays a pivotal role in user interface design, influencing readability and overall aesthetics. The "Mapika" UI Kit incorporates two carefully chosen fonts to achieve a modern and user-friendly look:

  • Nunito: This sans-serif font, available via Google Fonts, offers a clean and legible style that ensures content is easily readable on mobile screens. Its versatility makes it suitable for various design elements within the app.

  • Secular One: Adding a touch of uniqueness to the design, Secular One is used selectively to make specific text elements stand out. This font choice adds personality and visual interest to the app.

Images (Assets): Images are a critical component of any mobile app's visual appeal, and the "Mapika" UI Kit uses a selection of free and open-license images to enhance its overall design:

  • Electric Cars Parking Lot Charging: This image sets the tone for the app, showcasing electric vehicles charging in a parking lot. It conveys the essence of eco-friendly transportation and the app's primary function.

  • Electric Car Vector: The vector illustration of an electric car reinforces the app's focus on electric vehicle-related content.

  • Hand-Drawn "No Data" Illustration: This unique hand-drawn illustration serves as an attention-grabbing visual element, indicating situations where data may be unavailable.

  • Front Car Concept Illustration: The front car concept illustration adds a dynamic and engaging visual element to the app, making it more visually appealing.

  • 3D Electric Car Charging Station: This 3D image of an electric car at a charging station complements the app's core functionality, emphasizing the charging station aspect.

These images are thoughtfully integrated into the UI Kit to provide users with a visually stimulating and informative experience.

Icons: Icons are essential for conveying information quickly and efficiently in a mobile app. The "Mapika" UI Kit sources icons from two reputable free and open-icon websites:

  • SVGRepo: This resource offers a vast collection of SVG icons, ensuring that the app's iconography is diverse and suited to different contexts.

  • Iconfinder: Iconfinder provides high-quality icons that enhance the app's visual identity and provide users with intuitive cues.

By incorporating icons from these sources, the UI Kit ensures that every icon used is clear, cohesive, and aligned with the app's design language.

The UI Kit offers a comprehensive set of screens organized into several groups, each serving specific purposes within the app. These groups provide a clear structure for the app's functionality and user experience. Let's explore the screens in these groups:

1. Onboarding and Splash Screens:

  • Welcome Screen: The first impression of the app, welcoming users and setting the tone for their experience.
  • Introduction Screens: These screens introduce users to the app's features and functionality.
  • Get Started: A call-to-action screen prompting users to start using the app.
  • Splash Screen: Displayed briefly during app launch, featuring the "Mapika" logo and branding.

2. Authentication and User Profile:

  • Sign In: Allows users to log in to their accounts with a phone number and OTP verification.
  • Sign Up: Enables new users to create accounts by providing their phone number and other details.
  • Verification Successful: A confirmation screen indicating successful OTP verification.
  • Complete Your Profile: Prompts users to add their vehicle information for a personalised experience.
  • Edit Profile: Allows users to edit and update their profile information.
  • Change Password: Enables users to change their account passwords securely.

3. Home and Navigation:

  • Home Screen: The app's main dashboard, displaying nearby EV charging stations, filters, and a map view.
  • Navigation Bar: Provides easy access to different sections of the app, including Home, Favourites, My Bookings, and Profile.
  • Search: A screen allowing users to search for specific locations or EV charging stations.
  • Filters: Enables users to refine their search results based on various criteria.
  • Location Details: Displays detailed information about a selected location or charging station.
  • Charging Station Map View: A map view showing the locations of charging stations with color-coded indicators for availability.

4. Favorites and Booking:

  • Favourites: Allows users to save their favourite EV charging stations for quick access.
  • My Bookings: Displays a history of users' previous bookings and their details.
  • Booking Details: Provides in-depth information about a specific booking, including date, time, and station details.
  • Review List: Allows users to leave reviews and ratings for charging stations they've visited.
  • Add Review: The screen where users can write and submit their reviews.

5. Notifications and Alerts:

  • Alerts and Status: Notifies users about important updates, such as booking confirmations or station availability.
  • Get OTP: Provides users with a one-time password (OTP) for verification purposes.
  • Resend OTP: Allows users to request a new OTP if they haven't received one.
  • Verification Timer: Displays a countdown timer for OTP verification.

6. Vehicle Management:

  • Add Vehicle: Enables users to add their vehicle details for personalized recommendations.
  • Select Vehicle: Provides options for users to choose their vehicle type and model.
  • Edit Vehicle: Allows users to modify or update their vehicle information.

7. Booking and Reservation:

  • Select Charger: Users can choose the charger type and availability for their booking.
  • Select Payment Mode: Provides options for payment methods and preferences.
  • Summary: A screen summarising the booking details, including charging station, date, and time.
  • Booking Confirmation: Confirms the successful booking of a charging station.
  • Booking Cancelation: Allows users to cancel their bookings if necessary.

8. Settings and Help:

  • Settings: Provides access to various app settings, including language preferences and notification preferences.
  • Help Center: Offers users resources and assistance for using the app effectively.
  • Privacy Policy: Displays the app's privacy policy for user reference.
  • Contact Us: Provides contact information for user inquiries and support.

9. Additional Screens:

  • No Data Illustration: A screen to inform users when there is no data or results available.
  • Change Location: Allows users to change their current location or set a preferred location.
  • Payment Methods: Displays available payment methods and allows users to add new ones.
  • Logout: Enables users to log out of their accounts securely.
  • Extended License: A screen explaining the extended license terms and conditions.

These organised screen groups ensure that the "Mapika" Electric Vehicle Location Finder Mobile App UI Kit offers a cohesive and user-friendly experience, covering all essential aspects of EV charging station discovery, booking, and user management. Designers and developers can leverage these screens to create a feature-rich and visually appealing EV location finder app tailored to their users' needs.

In summary, the "Mapika" Electric Vehicle Location Finder Mobile App UI Kit is a thoughtfully crafted resource designed to offer a seamless and visually pleasing experience on mobile devices. From the choice of the iPhone 13 Mini's layout to the selection of fonts, images, and icons, every aspect of the UI Kit is carefully considered to enhance user engagement and usability. Designers and developers can leverage these design elements to create a cutting-edge and user-friendly EV location finder mobile app while maintaining a consistent and modern design throughout the entire application.

Components and Elements

Design Elements in the "Mapika" Electric Vehicle (EV) Location Finder Mobile App UI Kit are carefully crafted components that contribute to the app's visual appeal, usability, and overall user experience. These design elements provide consistency, scalability, and flexibility to designers and developers, making it easier to create a cohesive and engaging mobile app. Let's delve into the key design elements of the UI Kit:

1. Color Palette:

  • Primary Colors: The UI Kit primarily features a modern color palette, with a predominant use of shades of blue, green, and white. These colors evoke a sense of trust, environmental friendliness, and clarity.
  • Accent Colors: Complementary accent colors are strategically used to highlight buttons, icons, and interactive elements, ensuring they stand out for user attention.
  • Error and Validation Colors: Red is used to indicate errors or validation issues, such as incorrect input or missing fields, providing clear feedback to users.


2. Typography:

  • Font Choices: The UI Kit employs two distinct fonts to enhance readability and convey information effectively.
    • Nunito: Used for body text, providing a clean and easy-to-read style.
    • Secular One: Used for headings and titles, adding a touch of uniqueness and emphasis.

3. Icons:

  • Icon Styles: Icons are selected from two reputable sources, SVGRepo and IconFinder, ensuring a consistent and professional look throughout the app.
  • Purposeful Icons: Icons are thoughtfully chosen to represent various app functions, such as navigation, favorites, charging stations, and user profiles.


4. Buttons and Controls:

  • Button Styles: Buttons are designed with rounded corners and contrasting colors to make them visually distinct and inviting for user interaction.
  • Toggle Buttons: Toggle buttons are utilized for filters and preferences, allowing users to easily switch between options.

5. Cards and Panels:

  • Card Layouts: Information is often presented in card layouts, making it easy to group related content and provide a visually appealing presentation.
  • Drop Shadows: Subtle drop shadows under cards and panels provide a sense of depth and make them appear elevated, enhancing the overall user interface.

6. Input Fields and Forms:

  • Form Elements: Text input fields, checkboxes, and radio buttons are styled for consistency, making it intuitive for users to provide information and make selections.
  • Validation Feedback: Clear error messages and validation indicators are included to guide users in completing forms correctly.

7. Illustrations and Images:

  • Illustrations: The UI Kit features high-quality illustrations that complement the app's theme, including images of electric cars and charging stations.
  • License-Compliant Images: Images used in the UI Kit are sourced from free and open-license image repositories, ensuring they can be used legally without copyright concerns.

8. Navigation and Menus:

  • Navigation Bar: A clean and simple navigation bar at the bottom of the screen provides easy access to different app sections.
  • Hamburger Menu: In some screens, a hamburger menu icon is used to reveal additional navigation options.

These design elements collectively contribute to a visually appealing, user-friendly, and functional mobile app interface. Designers and developers can leverage these elements to create a seamless and engaging experience for users of the "Mapika" Electric Vehicle Location Finder app, while maintaining a cohesive and professional aesthetic throughout the application.
